These are not bad ideas. They should wait until Drop 001 product proof, fulfillment readiness, finance math, and checkout readiness are clear.

1. Creating more agents
   - Current team is enough: Orchestrator, Chief of Staff, Website, Marketing, Finance, Brand QA, Product/Supplier, Operations/CX.

2. Building a CEO agent
   - Jace remains owner/founder and final decision maker.

3. Extra products beyond Tee / Hoodie / Hat
   - Drop 001 is exactly three pieces.

4. New brand direction / logo changes
   - Only revisit if Jace explicitly requests it.

5. Community expansion plans
   - Keep Discord/community framing clean, but do not overbuild community before product and checkout are ready.

6. Full SEO/content machine
   - Journal/about/community pages can wait until launch basics are stable.

7. Influencer/seeding program
   - Wait until product sample/proof and fulfillment expectations are clear.

8. Aggressive paid ads
   - Prepare creative, but do not spend until landing path, proof claims, and finance guardrails are ready.

9. Advanced analytics dashboards
   - Basic conversion/event verification first; deeper dashboards after traffic starts.

10. Packaging as a separate agent
   - Keep packaging under Operations/CX for now.

## Working Rule

If it does not help product proof, fulfillment readiness, launch math, conversion path, or required Jace decisions, park it.
