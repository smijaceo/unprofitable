# UNPROFITABLE Discord Channel Mapping

## Orchestrator

- Channel: bot DMs unless otherwise instructed
- Purpose: main coordination, routing, blocker tracking, and launch summary delivery.

## Chief of Staff

- Agent: `unprofitable-chief-of-staff-agent`
- Suggested channel name: `#unprofitable-chief-of-staff`
- Channel ID: `1513628295026180187`
- Purpose: Jace clarity, weekly priorities, decisions, blockers, follow-up, do-later list.

Do not use Launch Decisions as the Chief of Staff channel.

## Website Designer

- Agent: `unprofitable-website-designer`
- Channel ID: `1512103560597213224`
- Purpose: website layout, homepage, shop page, product pages, CTAs, mobile UX, trust sections, product-page presentation, and drop-list conversion.

## Marketing

- Agent: `unprofitable-marketing-specialist`
- Channel ID: `1512235449463210127`
- Purpose: ads, hooks, UGC prompts, Meta ad copy, creative testing, campaign angles, and ad-to-landing-page match.

## Finance

- Agent: `finance-operator-agent`
- Channel ID: `1513264220073099345`
- Purpose: costs, pricing, COGS, margins, ROI, break-even, cash flow, inventory risk, bundle economics, and ad-spend risk.

## Brand QA

- Agent: `unprofitable-brand-qa-agent`
- Channel ID: `1513626486240972965`
- Purpose: brand consistency, tone, visual rules, approved/banned phrasing, off-brand review, and public-facing consistency.

## Product / Supplier

- Agent: `unprofitable-product-supplier-agent`
- Channel ID: `1513626510337114112`
- Purpose: blanks, suppliers, garment specs, GSM/fabric weight, materials, sizing, samples, print/embroidery, wash testing, and product proof.

## Operations / CX

- Agent: `unprofitable-operations-cx-agent`
- Channel ID: `1513626545728655360`
- Purpose: fulfillment, packaging, shipping, returns, exchanges, damaged items, support templates, FAQ, and launch-day operations.

## Launch Decisions

- Channel ID: `1513626852462170253`
- Purpose: only decisions that need Jace approval. This is not an agent channel and should not become general chatter.

Items that belong here:

- Supplier choice
- Final product pricing
- Final product quantities
- Final sample approval
- Paid ad spend approval
- Checkout launch approval
- Shipping/return policy approval
- Major website/brand changes
- Product claims that need Jace sign-off

## Working Rule

```text
Orchestrator manages agents. Chief of Staff manages clarity.
```
