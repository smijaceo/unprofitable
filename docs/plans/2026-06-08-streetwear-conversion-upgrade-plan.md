# Streetwear Brand Conversion Upgrade Plan

## Goal
Upgrade wearunprofitable.com from a strong preview into a credible premium streetwear drop site that can convert ad traffic into drop-list signups while checkout remains closed.

## Scope
- Strengthen the shop hub with launch sequence, early-access benefits, product proof promise, limited first-run language, founder/world context, and FAQ.
- Upgrade product pages with Product Proof, Fit & Sizing, model-reference placeholders, shipping/returns preview, and complete-the-uniform CTAs.
- Rewrite policy pages so they feel intentional before commerce opens.
- Keep all CTAs early-access/list based until checkout is live.

## Guardrails
- Do not redesign the identity from scratch.
- Do not fake scarcity or final specs.
- Do not use Add to Cart / Buy Now / Checkout CTAs publicly.
- Keep black-and-white premium streetwear direction.
- Buyer-facing language only; no internal build terms.

## Verification
- Scan for internal/forbidden terms.
- Parse HTML references and JSON-LD.
- Local browser console and visual QA.
- Mobile screenshots at 390px and 430px.
- Commit, push, and live cache-busted verification.
