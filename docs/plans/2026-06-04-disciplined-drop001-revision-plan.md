# DISCIPLINED / 001 Website Revision Plan

Date: 2026-06-04

## Goal

Revise the UNPROFITABLE launch site so it matches the actual first drop instead of the temporary “Built Before Paid” positioning.

## Brand Direction

UNPROFITABLE is premium monochrome trading streetwear. It should feel like a luxury editorial streetwear launch page, not a founder merch page, coaching funnel, crypto store, meme brand, SaaS page, or trading-guru website.

Core language:

```text
DROP 001: DISCIPLINED / 001
PROCESS OVER OUTCOME
UNPROFITABLE TODAY. PROFITABLE TOMORROW.
```

Community angle: accountability and discipline, not signals.

## Drop Architecture

Exactly three pieces:

1. DISCIPLINED Oversized Tee
2. DISCIPLINED Heavyweight Hoodie
3. DISCIPLINED Structured Hat

## Visual Requirements

- Black and white only.
- Heavyweight black garments.
- White U/P monogram logo.
- Bottom-left garment patch on shirt and hoodie.
- Big curved DISCIPLINED typography across upper back and sleeves.
- Small supporting line: PROCESS OVER OUTCOME.
- Hat uses arched DISCIPLINED front embroidery, small UNPROFITABLE back embroidery, and U/P side embroidery.
- No charts, candles, red/green, crypto, P&L screenshots, strategy-selling, or fake guru imagery.

## Homepage Structure

1. Hero with actual tee/hoodie/hat imagery.
2. Featured Drop with exactly three product cards.
3. Design Details / Garment Code section.
4. Manifesto: discipline before profitability.
5. Discord/community: accountability, not signals.
6. Drop list / early access signup.
7. Footer with disclaimer.
