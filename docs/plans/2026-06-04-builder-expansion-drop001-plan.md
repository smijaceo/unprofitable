# UNPROFITABLE Builder Expansion + Drop 001 Website Implementation Plan

> **For Hermes:** Keep the website premium, black/white, merch-first, and honest. Expand beyond traders without becoming generic hustle/guru merch.

**Goal:** Reposition the homepage from trader-only recovery arc to a broader builder-before-profit brand and redesign Drop 001 around exactly one shirt, one hoodie, and one hat.

**Architecture:** Keep the static site and existing monochrome system. Update homepage copy, SEO/structured data, waitlist options, product cards, crawler docs, and legal placeholders. Preserve current badge/monogram assets and mobile CTA behavior.

**Tech Stack:** Static HTML/CSS/JS, local assets, JSON-LD, sitemap/robots/llms, black/white premium streetwear visual system.

---

## Strategic Positioning

UNPROFITABLE should now mean: premium streetwear for people building through the phase before it pays.

Audience expands to:
- traders reviewing losses and building discipline
- founders and business owners reinvesting before they pay themselves
- creators publishing before monetization
- operators learning that revenue is not profit

Public-facing line:

```text
For the ones building before it pays.
```

## Drop 001 Product Plan

Keep the first drop tight and believable:

1. `Built Before Paid Shirt` — daily black shirt for founders, creators, traders, and operators still reinvesting into the climb.
2. `Drawdown Season Hoodie` — heavyweight black hoodie for the months where the business, account, or project costs more than it pays.
3. `Risk Managed Hat` — low-profile black hat for people who understand survival is part of the edge.

No sticker pack in Drop 001. No fake scarcity claims. Product CTAs remain preview/coming soon until checkout is real.

## Website Changes Implemented

- Hero copy broadened from trader-only to traders/founders/creators/business owners.
- Recovery board reframed as `Builder Arc / Uniform Code` with Risk → Review → Reinvest → Return.
- Waitlist copy and select options changed to one shirt, one hoodie, one hat.
- Product grid changed from four products to three focused product cards.
- Manifesto expanded to founders, creators, operators, and traders.

- Canonical, sitemap, robots, JSON-LD, README, manifest, and llms.txt updated to `wearunprofitable.com` and builder-before-profit positioning.
