**Status:** Needs decision

**Status:** Needs Product/Ops decision

## Brand QA Pass

Good customer-facing lines:

- “No mystery checkout.”
- “Final terms will be live before payment.”
- “We’d rather you buy the right piece than guess.”
- “If we shipped the wrong item, we’ll handle it plainly.”

Do not publish:

- “Hassle-free returns” unless the policy genuinely supports it.
- “Fast shipping” until carrier/processing is tested.
- “Easy exchanges” unless inventory/reship cost is approved.
- “Limited forever” unless quantity/restock decision is real.
