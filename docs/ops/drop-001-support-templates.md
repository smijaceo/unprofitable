# Drop 001 Support Templates

**Owner lane:** Operations / Customer Experience  
**Brand QA rule:** minimal, human, direct. No guru language. No fake warmth. No overpromising.  
**Support inbox:** `drop@wearunprofitable.com`

---

## Template Rules

- Keep replies short.
- Use the customer’s name if available.
- Never promise unapproved shipping timelines, free returns, refunds, exchanges, or restocks.
- If checkout is not live, say that clearly.
- If UNPROFITABLE caused the issue, own it plainly.
- Always include the next step.

## 12. Brand QA Notes

Approved tone:

- “We’ll take a look.”
- “No mystery checkout.”
- “We’d rather you buy the right piece than guess.”
- “If we shipped the wrong item, we’ll handle it plainly.”

Avoid:

- “We are deeply sorry for any inconvenience caused.”
- “Your satisfaction is our top priority.”
- “Hassle-free returns” unless the policy truly supports it.
- “Ships fast” until shipping is tested and approved.
- “Limited to the chosen few” or fake scarcity.
