Drop 001 / Privacy

Privacy.

UNPROFITABLE only asks for the information needed to run the Drop 001 list, send launch updates, and understand which pieces people care about. No sensitive information belongs in the signup form.

What We Collect.

When you join the Drop List, we collect your email address and any optional product-interest or audience choices you submit. The site may also record basic page/source context so we know which page or product card led to the signup.

How We Use It.

  • Send Drop 001 launch timing and early-access updates.
  • Share final size charts, product proof, material notes, and shipping details.
  • Understand demand across the tee, hoodie, hat, and bundles.
  • Send restock or second-run updates if they happen.

What We Do Not Do.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not ask for card numbers or payment details through the Drop List. If checkout goes live, payment information is handled by the selected checkout provider, not by this static signup page.

Your Choices.

You can ask to be removed from the Drop List or update your email preferences by contacting drop@wearunprofitable.com. If email campaigns begin, unsubscribe instructions will be included in the message.
